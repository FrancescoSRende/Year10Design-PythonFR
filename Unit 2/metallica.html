Metallica

The biggest and most commercially successful thrash metal band, and one of, if not the, most popular metal bands of all time, Metallica's domination over heavy metal spanned over a decade and incorporated numerous genres. From their beginnings with their punk-influenced debut Kill 'Em All, to the more melodic and refined Ride the Lightning (which saw them beginning to experiment with power ballads in Fade to Black), to what was undoubtedly the most infleuntial thrash metal album of all time, Master of Puppets, even to a more commercial yet nonetheless powerful shift in sound via their selftitled Metallica (the Black Album), Metallica have proven themselves to be a very versatile and technically proficient band. They were always willing to experiment with many different sounds, including many bass-prominent instrumentals, such as Orion, the Call of Ktulu and Cliff Burton's greatest bass solo, Anesthesia (Pulling Teeth).
