Pantera

Pantera was very much the band that brought a swift end to the glam metal era of the 80s. Their brand of down-tuned, slowed-down thrash metal was a large influence on the later NWOAHM (New Wave of American Heavy Metal). Granted, they were once one of those very glam metal bands, but all of that changed with the introduction of their iconic singer, Phil Anselmo. They released one more glam album with Anselmo before doing a complete 180 and coming out with one of the most successful metal releases of the 90s, Cowboys From Hell. This album featured breakneck riffing, shred-filled solos and a more prominent role from both the bassist and drummer than the average metal band at the time.
