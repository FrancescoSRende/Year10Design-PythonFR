Children of Bodom Reviews

Follow the Reaper

Sputnikmusic: 5/5

"...are there any recent albums that could bask in the same utterly metallic glory as the aforementioned albums? You could make a case for Children of Bodom's Follow the Reaper. Released in 2001, it was the band's third full length album, and by far their best... Children of Bodom's Follow the Reaper represents the height of the extreme power metal genre, and a definite classic."

Francesco Rende: 10/10

"Follow the Reaper presents a perfect blend of addictive melodic hooks, epic neoclassical solos and extreme metal headbanging."
