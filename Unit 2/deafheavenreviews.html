Deafheaven Reviews

Sunbather

Francesco Rende: 10/10

"Every single track is packed full of beautiful psychedelic melodies and chords, whether via soothing clean guitars or relentless, screaming distorted guitars. Each song is an opportunity to spiral out into somewhere new, and they are all beautifully experimental and without flaw."

Pitchfork: 8.9/10

"Deafheaven's second album is an epic mix of intense black metal and chiming post-rock... a record that finds Deafheaven living up to and then surpassing expectations. Basically, they've learned how to take the sounds they've dreamed up out of their heads so we can hear them, too."
