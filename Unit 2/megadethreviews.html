Megadeth Reviews

Rust In Peace

Allmusic: 4.5/5

"...easily Megadeth's strongest musical effort... Mustaine accentuates the progressive tendencies of his compositions, producing rhythmically complex, technically challenging thrash suites that he and Friedman burn through with impeccable execution and jaw-dropping skill."

Francesco Rende: 10/10

"Every single song on this album is a flawless, headbanging masterpiece. Each track presents new, infinitely catchy riffs and epic, addictive, shred-filled, prog-tinged solos."
