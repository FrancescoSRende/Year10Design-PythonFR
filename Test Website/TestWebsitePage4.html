Good Songs

Ghost Reveries - Opeth

Opeth's 2005 return to their traditional technical death metal style is packed full of incredible riffs, headbanging anthems and beautiful melodic passages.

L'Enfant Sauvage - Gojira

Gojira's explosive 2012 release promises just as much bone-crushingly heavy riffing and just as many powerful lyrics alongside a newfound talent for writing amazing melodic riffs and lead parts.
